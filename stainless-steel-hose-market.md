<p><p>Stainless steel hose market is divided into two main types - flexible hoses and corrugated hoses. Flexible hoses are designed to bend and move easily, making them ideal for applications where flexibility is required. On the other hand, corrugated hoses have a series of ridges or folds which allow for increased flexibility and durability. Both types are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing where they are utilized for applications requiring high pressure or extreme temperatures.</p></p>
