<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Sight Market is divided into two main types: Image Intensifier and Thermal Image. Image Intensifier technology enhances available light to provide a clear view of targets in low-light conditions. Thermal Image technology, on the other hand, detects heat signatures to create an image, allowing users to see targets even in complete darkness. Both types of optical sights are widely used in military, law enforcement, hunting, and outdoor activities for improved accuracy and target acquisition in various scenarios.</p></p>
