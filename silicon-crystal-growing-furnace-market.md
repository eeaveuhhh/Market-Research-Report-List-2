<p><strong>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski(CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone(FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon crystal growing furnaces are categorized into two main types: Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace and Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace. CZ method furnaces are widely used for industrial production of silicon crystals due to their high efficiency and large crystal size capabilities. On the other hand, FZ method furnaces are preferred for research and specialty applications due to their ability to produce ultra-high purity silicon crystals with minimal contamination. Both types of furnaces play a crucial role in the production of silicon wafers for various electronic devices.</p></p>
