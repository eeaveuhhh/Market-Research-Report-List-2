<p><p>A Capacitive Liquid Level Switch is a type of sensor that is used to detect the level of liquid in a tank or container. It works on the principle of capacitance, where the sensor sends out an electrical signal and measures the change in capacitance when the liquid comes in contact with the sensor. This change in capacitance is used to determine the level of liquid present.</p><p>The Capacitive Liquid Level Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period.
