<p><strong>The NFC Product Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NFC Controller</li><li>NFC Tag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NFC market types include NFC controllers and NFC tags. NFC controllers are integrated circuits that enable communication between devices and NFC tags. These controllers are essential components in NFC-enabled devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other electronics. On the other hand, NFC tags are small, passive devices that can store information and communicate with NFC-enabled devices when in close proximity. The NFC tag market includes various types such as stickers, cards, and key fobs, which are used for applications like payment, access control, and marketing.</p></p>
