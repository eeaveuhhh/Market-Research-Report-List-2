<p><p>Failure analysis market is segmented into Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM), Focused Ion Beam (FIB) System, Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM), and Dual Beam System. SEM is used for high-resolution imaging, while FIB is employed for cross-sectioning and material removal applications. TEM is utilized for ultra-high-resolution imaging and analytical techniques. Dual Beam System combines the capabilities of both SEM and FIB for advanced material characterization and cross-sectioning. Each type of system caters to different requirements in the failure analysis process.</p></p>
