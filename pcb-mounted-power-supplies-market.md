<p><p>PCB mounted power supplies can be categorized into two main types: adjustable and fixed. Adjustable power supplies allow users to manually adjust the output voltage as needed, providing flexibility and customization for different applications. On the other hand, fixed power supplies have a set output voltage that cannot be adjusted. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different purposes based on the level of control and stability required for the specific electronic devices or systems they are powering.</p></p>
