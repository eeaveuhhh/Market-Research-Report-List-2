<p><p>Fiber optical couplers are devices used to split or combine optical signals in fiber optic systems. The market types include Y Coupler, which splits optical signals evenly; T Coupler, which splits signals unequally in a T shape; Star C Coupler, which combines multiple signals into one common output; and Tree Coupler, which splits signals multiple times like branches on a tree. Each type serves a specific function in fiber optic networks, catering to different signal distribution needs.</p></p>
