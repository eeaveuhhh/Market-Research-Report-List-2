<p><ul><li>Tactile Switch</li><li>Rotary Switch</li><li>Encoder Switch</li><li>Toggle Switch</li><li>Push Switch</li><li>Detect Switch</li><li>Micro Switch</li><li>Dip Switch</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electromechanical switches encompass a variety of types that cater to different needs. Tactile switches provide tactile feedback when pressed, while rotary switches rotate to select options. Encoder switches convert rotary motion into digital signals. Toggle switches have a lever to toggle between two positions. Push switches are pressed to make or break connections. Detect switches are sensitive to external forces. Micro switches are small and precise. DIP switches have multiple independent switches in a single package. Other types include rocker switches, slide switches, and membrane switches.</p></p>
