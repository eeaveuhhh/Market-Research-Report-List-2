<p><ul><li>Volar Plates</li><li>Dorsal Plates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distal radius plates are orthopedic implants used in the treatment of fractures in the distal radius bone of the forearm. Volar plates are types of distal radius plates that are placed on the palmar side of the wrist, while dorsal plates are placed on the back side of the wrist. Volar plates are more commonly used for fractures on the volar side of the distal radius, providing stable fixation. Dorsal plates, on the other hand, are used for fractures on the dorsal side of the distal radius to support proper alignment and healing.</p></p>
