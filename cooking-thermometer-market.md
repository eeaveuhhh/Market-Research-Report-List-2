<p><strong>The Cooking Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instant-Read</li><li>In-Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cooking Thermometer market consists of two main types: Instant-Read and In-Probe thermometers. Instant-Read thermometers provide quick temperature readings, making them ideal for checking the temperature of food during cooking or grilling. In-Probe thermometers are designed to be inserted into the food being cooked, providing continuous temperature monitoring without the need to open the oven or grill. Both types of thermometers are essential tools for ensuring that food is cooked to the proper temperature and is safe to eat.</p></p>
