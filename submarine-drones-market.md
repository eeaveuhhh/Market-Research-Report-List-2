<p><p>Submarine drones are unmanned underwater vehicles used for various applications such as ocean exploration, defense, and research. There are two main types of submarine drones on the market: tethered and wireless. Tethered submarine drones are connected to a base station by a physical cable, allowing for continuous power and data transmission. In contrast, wireless submarine drones operate autonomously without a physical connection, relying on wireless communication technology for data transfer. Both types have their unique advantages and are used in different scenarios based on requirements.</p></p>
