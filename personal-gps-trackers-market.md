<p><p>Personal GPS trackers come in two main types: SIM GPS trackers and SIM-free GPS trackers. SIM GPS trackers require a SIM card to function and they rely on cellular networks for tracking and communication. On the other hand, SIM-free GPS trackers do not require a SIM card and often use Bluetooth or WiFi technology for tracking and communication. Both types of GPS trackers have their own advantages and are suitable for different types of users depending on their usage requirements and preferences.</p></p>
