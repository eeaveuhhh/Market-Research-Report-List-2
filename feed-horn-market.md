<p><strong>The Feed Horn Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rectangular</li><li>Circular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Feed horns are essential components in satellite communication systems. They come in two main market types: rectangular and circular. Rectangular feed horns are commonly used in applications where higher gain and narrower beamwidth are required, such as long-distance satellite communication. Circular feed horns, on the other hand, are preferred for applications where wider beamwidth is needed, such as broadcasting and weather satellite communication. Both types offer specific advantages and are chosen based on the requirements of the specific communication system.</p></p>
