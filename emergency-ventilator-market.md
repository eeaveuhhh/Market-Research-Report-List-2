<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Emergency Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volume-controlled ventilation modes</li><li>Pressure-controlled ventilation modes</li><li>Spontaneous breathing (non-invasive)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volume-controlled ventilation modes deliver a set tidal volume to the patient regardless of changes in airway pressure. Pressure-controlled ventilation modes allow for control over the peak inspiratory pressure delivered to the patient. Spontaneous breathing, also known as non-invasive ventilation, includes methods such as CPAP and BiPAP that support a patient's breathing without the need for intubation. These different types of emergency ventilators cater to varying patient needs and conditions, providing flexibility and individualized care.</p></p>
