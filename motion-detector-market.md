<p><ul><li>Accelerometer</li><li>Gyroscope</li><li>Magnetometer</li><li>Combo Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motion detector market is divided into various types based on the technology used, including accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and combo sensors. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration, gyroscopes measure rotational speed, and magnetometers detect magnetic fields. Combo sensors combine two or more of these technologies to provide more accurate and comprehensive motion detection capabilities. Each type has specific applications, with accelerometers commonly used in smartphones for screen orientation, gyroscopes in drones for stabilization, and magnetometers in navigation systems for direction sensing.</p></p>
