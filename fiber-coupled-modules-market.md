<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fiber Coupled Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitter-Based FCM</li><li>Bar-Based FCM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emitter-Based FCMs are fiber-coupled modules that use individual diode laser emitters to deliver laser light into a fiber optic cable. These modules are commonly used in applications requiring high power densities and precise control of the laser beam. On the other hand, Bar-Based FCMs utilize laser diode bars, which consist of multiple emitters mounted on a single substrate. Bar-Based FCMs are preferred for applications requiring higher power levels and better beam quality compared to Emitter-Based FCMs.</p></p>
