There are two main types of constant speed propellers: hydraulic propellers and electric propellers. Hydraulic propellers use hydraulic fluid to adjust the pitch of the propeller blades, while electric propellers use electric motors. Both types of propellers offer precise control over engine speed, enhancing overall aircraft performance and efficiency.</p></p>
