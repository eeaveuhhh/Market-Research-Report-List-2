<p><p>Electrical air heaters come in various types to suit different heating requirements. Immersion heaters are designed to be immersed directly in the fluid to be heated. Tubular heaters are versatile and can be used for both convection and radiant heating. Circulation heaters are ideal for heating air in enclosed spaces. Band heaters wrap around cylindrical surfaces for uniform heating. Strip heaters are flat and can be easily mounted on surfaces. Coil heaters provide quick and efficient heating for small spaces. Other types of air heaters include infrared heaters, duct heaters, and duct furnaces.</p></p>
