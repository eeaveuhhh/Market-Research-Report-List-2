<p><p>Duodenal stents are medical devices used to treat blockages in the duodenum, a part of the small intestine. There are two main types of duodenal stents available in the market: Polymer Duodenal Stent and Metal Duodenal Stent. </p><p>Polymer Duodenal Stents are made of biocompatible materials that gradually dissolve in the body, offering temporary relief from blockages. Metal Duodenal Stents, on the other hand, are made of non-biodegradable materials like stainless steel or nitinol, providing a more permanent solution for duodenal obstructions. Both types of stents have their own unique advantages and applications in clinical settings.</p></p>
