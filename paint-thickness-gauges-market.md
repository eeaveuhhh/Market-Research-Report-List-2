<p><ul><li>Magnetic and Eddy Current Tech</li><li>Ultrasonic Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paint thickness gauges are available in three main types in the market - Magnetic, Eddy Current, and Ultrasonic Technology. Magnetic gauges are based on the principle of magnetic induction, measuring the thickness of non-magnetic coatings on magnetic substrates. Eddy Current gauges use electromagnetic induction to measure non-conductive coatings on conductive substrates. Ultrasonic technology gauges use sound waves to measure coating thickness on various substrates. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
