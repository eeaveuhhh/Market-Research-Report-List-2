<p><ul><li>Piston Aerators</li><li>Diaphragms Aerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Septic aerators are classified into two main types: piston aerators and diaphragm aerators. </p><p>Piston aerators use a piston mechanism to push air into the septic tank, creating bubbles that help break down waste. On the other hand, diaphragm aerators use a flexible diaphragm to create pressure changes that push air into the tank. </p><p>Both types of aerators serve the same purpose of introducing oxygen into the septic system to promote the growth of beneficial bacteria that break down waste efficiently.</p></p>
