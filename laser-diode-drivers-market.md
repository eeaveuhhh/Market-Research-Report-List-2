<p><p>Laser diode drivers are used to power laser diodes and control their output. Seed laser diode drivers are used to initiate the lasing process by providing low-level continuous power, while pulsed laser diode drivers deliver short bursts of high power for applications such as material processing or medical procedures. CW (continuous wave) laser diode drivers provide a steady, constant power output for applications like telecommunications or scientific research. Each type of laser diode driver caters to specific requirements and applications within the market.</p></p>
