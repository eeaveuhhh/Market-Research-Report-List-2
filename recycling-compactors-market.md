Large compactors have a pressing force between 10-80 tons, designed for larger quantities of waste. Heavy-duty compactors have a pressing force greater than 80 tons, ideal for industrial or commercial use where large volumes of waste need to be compacted efficiently. Each type of compactor is designed to cater to specific recycling needs.</p></p>
