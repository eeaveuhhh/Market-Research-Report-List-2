<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) accelerometers are classified into two main types based on the number of axes they measure: Mono-axial type and Multi-axial type. Mono-axial accelerometers measure acceleration along one specific axis, providing precise data for single-direction movements. Multi-axial accelerometers measure acceleration along multiple axes simultaneously, offering comprehensive data for complex movements and vibrations. Depending on the application requirements, users can choose between these two types to accurately measure acceleration in different directions.</p></p>
