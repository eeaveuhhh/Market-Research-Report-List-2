<p><strong>The Kale Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Dry</li><li>Freeze Dry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kale powder is available in two main types in the market: air dry and freeze dry. Air dry kale powder is made by exposing kale leaves to warm, circulating air to remove moisture. This method is cost-effective but may result in loss of some nutrients due to heat exposure. On the other hand, freeze dry kale powder involves freezing the leaves and then removing the moisture under vacuum conditions. This process helps to retain more nutrients and results in a higher quality product, but it is more expensive than air drying.</p></p>
