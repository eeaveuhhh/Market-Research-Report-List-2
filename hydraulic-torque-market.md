<p><p>Hydraulic torque market encompasses two main types of tools: Drive Hydraulic Wrench and Hollow Hydraulic Wrench. Drive Hydraulic Wrenches are used for applications where standard socket wrenches are not suitable due to limited access or high torque requirements. They provide precise torque control and are ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications. Hollow Hydraulic Wrenches are designed for applications where the bolt passes through the tool, allowing for continuous rotation without having to reposition the tool. They are commonly used in industries such as construction, mining, and oil & gas.</p></p>
