<p><p>Atomic clock market is divided into three main types: Rubidium Atomic Clock, CSAC/Cs Beam Atomic Clock, and Hydrogen Maser Atomic Clock. Rubidium Atomic Clock uses rubidium gas to keep time accurately, while CSAC/Cs Beam Atomic Clock uses cesium atoms for precise timekeeping. Hydrogen Maser Atomic Clock is the most accurate type, using hydrogen atoms to measure time at an extremely high level of precision. Each type of atomic clock caters to different applications and industries, offering varying levels of accuracy and performance.</p></p>
