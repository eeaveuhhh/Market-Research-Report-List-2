<p><p>Nano Quantum Dots (QD) market can be categorized into three main types based on the materials used: II-VI semiconductors, III-V semiconductors, and Silicon (Si). II-VI semiconductors, such as CdSe, offer high quantum yield and stability. III-V semiconductors, like InP, provide unique optical properties suitable for applications in electronics and photonics. Silicon QDs are gaining popularity due to their compatibility with existing silicon-based technology. Each type of QD material offers different advantages and applications in the rapidly growing nano quantum dots market.</p></p>
