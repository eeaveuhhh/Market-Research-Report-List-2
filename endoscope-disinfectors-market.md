Single chamber disinfectors have a single chamber for processing one endoscope at a time, making them suitable for smaller healthcare facilities. On the other hand, multi chamber disinfectors have multiple chambers for processing several endoscopes simultaneously, which is more efficient for larger healthcare facilities with high endoscope usage.</p></p>
