<p><p>Thermopile Infrared Temperature Sensors are available in two main market types: Through Hole Type and SMD Type. Through Hole Type sensors are designed to be mounted through holes in a circuit board, providing a sturdy mechanical connection. On the other hand, SMD Type sensors are surface-mounted devices that are soldered directly onto the surface of the circuit board. Both types offer accurate temperature sensing capabilities and are used in various applications such as industrial monitoring, automotive systems, and medical devices.</p></p>
