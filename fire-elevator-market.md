<p><strong>The Fire Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split Type</li><li>Straight Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire elevator markets can be categorized into two main types: Split Type and Straight Type. Split Type fire elevators have separate hoist mechanisms and motor rooms, allowing for easier installation in buildings with space constraints. On the other hand, Straight Type fire elevators have all components contained within a single shaft, making them more compact and efficient in larger buildings. Both types are designed to provide reliable and safe vertical transportation in case of emergencies, with variations available to suit different building layouts and requirements.</p></p>
