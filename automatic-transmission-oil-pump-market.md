<p><p>Automatic Transmission Oil Pump Market includes various types such as Internal Gear Oil Pump, Rotary Oil Pump, and Blade Oil Pump. Internal Gear Oil Pump operates by two internal gears that rotate and create suction to draw in oil. Rotary Oil Pump consists of rotary blades that rotate to pump oil. Blade Oil Pump utilizes blades that move back and forth within the pump to circulate oil. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different applications within the automotive industry.</p></p>
