<p><p>Disk lasers are categorized into three market types based on their power output: Low Power, Mid Power, and High Power. Low power disk lasers typically have an output of less than 1 kW and are commonly used in applications such as marking and engraving. Mid-power disk lasers have outputs between 1-3 kW and are used in cutting and welding applications. High-power disk lasers have outputs greater than 3 kW and find use in heavy-duty industrial cutting and welding processes.</p></p>
