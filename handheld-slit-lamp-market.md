<p><p>A handheld slit lamp is a portable ophthalmic device used by eye care professionals for examining the anterior segment of the eye. It provides a magnified view of the eye structures, allowing for detailed assessment of the cornea, anterior chamber, iris, and lens. The handheld slit lamp is compact, lightweight, and easy to use, making it ideal for use in remote or emergency settings where a traditional slit lamp may not be available.</p><p>The global handheld slit lamp market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with the market expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.00% during the forecast period. <p><p>The handheld slit lamp market consists of two main types: monocular and binocular handheld slit lamp devices. Monocular handheld slit lamps are equipped with a single eyepiece for viewing the eye, making them lightweight and portable. On the other hand, binocular handheld slit lamps have two eyepieces for a more detailed and accurate examination of the eye. Both types offer the convenience of handheld operation, making them popular choices for ophthalmologists and optometrists for on-the-go eye examinations.</p></p>
