<p><p>Intelligent bearingless rotary encoders are devices that measure and record the rotational movement of a shaft in various applications. The two main types of intelligent bearingless rotary encoders are absolute rotary encoders and incremental rotary encoders. </p><p>Absolute rotary encoders provide the exact position of the shaft at any given time, making them ideal for applications where precise positioning is crucial. Incremental rotary encoders measure relative movement of the shaft, providing information on direction and speed of rotation. Both types play a vital role in industries such as robotics, manufacturing, and automation.</p></p>
