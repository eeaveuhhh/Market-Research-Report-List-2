<p><p>Infrared quartz lamps are categorized into three types based on their wavelength ranges: Near Infrared, Medium Infrared, and Far Infrared. Near Infrared lamps emit shorter wavelengths, providing localized heating for specific applications. Medium Infrared lamps have slightly longer wavelengths, offering a balance between heating and penetration depth. Far Infrared lamps emit longer wavelengths that penetrate deeper into tissues, making them ideal for therapeutic and wellness purposes. Each type of lamp serves unique purposes depending on the desired heating and penetration levels.</p></p>
