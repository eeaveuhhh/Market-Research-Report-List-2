<p><p>Snowmelt systems are two main types: hydronic heating systems and electric snowmelt systems. Hydronic heating systems use hot water flowing through tubes under the surface to melt snow and ice. Electric snowmelt systems use electric coils or cables installed under the surface to generate heat and melt snow. Both types offer efficient and convenient solutions for melting snow and ice on driveways, walkways, and other outdoor surfaces, increasing safety and reducing the need for manual snow removal.</p></p>
