<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Switch</li><li>Static Pressure Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm Level Switches consist of two main types in the market: Differential Pressure Switch and Static Pressure Switch. Differential Pressure Switches utilize the difference in pressure between two points to detect liquid levels, while Static Pressure Switches rely on a set pressure threshold to actuate. Both types are widely used in various industries such as petrochemical, oil and gas, and water treatment, offering reliable and precise level detection for liquid substances. Each type caters to specific applications based on the requirements of the system.</p></p>
