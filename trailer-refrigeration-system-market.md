<p><p>Trailer refrigeration systems are classified into two main types: single temperature and multi-temperature. Single temperature systems are designed to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the trailer, making them suitable for transporting a single type of product at a specific temperature. On the other hand, multi-temperature systems are capable of maintaining multiple temperature zones within the same trailer, allowing for the transportation of a variety of products with different temperature requirements. Each type caters to different needs in the cold chain logistics industry.</p></p>
