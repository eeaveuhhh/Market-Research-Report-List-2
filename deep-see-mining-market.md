<p><p>Deep Sea Mining involves extracting valuable minerals from the ocean floor. There are three main market types in deep sea mining: Cobalt Rich Crusts (CRC), Manganese Nodules, and Seafloor Massive Sulphides (SMS). CRC contain high concentrations of cobalt, nickel, and other valuable minerals. Manganese Nodules are potato-sized rocks containing manganese, nickel, copper, and cobalt. SMS are formed at hydrothermal vents, containing high grades of copper, gold, silver, and other minerals. Each market type offers unique opportunities for mining companies seeking to extract valuable resources from the deep sea.</p></p>
