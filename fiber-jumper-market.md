<p><p>Fiber jumper market types include Single-mode and Multimode options. Single-mode jumpers are designed for long-distance transmission over large networks, providing high bandwidth and low signal loss. Multimode jumpers are better suited for short-distance applications within buildings or local area networks, offering lower bandwidth but at a more cost-effective price point. Both options have their advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs of the network being created.</p></p>
