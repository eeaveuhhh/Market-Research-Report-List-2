<p><p>Active RFID Readers are available in two main types; Wireless Active RFID Readers and Wired Active RFID Readers. Wireless Active RFID Readers use radio frequency technology to communicate with RFID tags without the need for physical connection. They offer flexibility and portability for tracking assets or people. On the other hand, Wired Active RFID Readers require a physical connection, typically through Ethernet or USB, for data transfer. They provide a more stable and secure connection but may be less convenient for mobile applications.</p></p>
