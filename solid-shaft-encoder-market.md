<p><strong>The Solid Shaft Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Solid Shaft Encoder</li><li>Absolute Solid Shaft Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid shaft encoders are used for measuring rotation and angular position in various industrial applications. Incremental solid shaft encoders provide relative position information by counting the number of pulses generated as the shaft rotates. On the other hand, absolute solid shaft encoders provide absolute position information, allowing the exact position to be determined even after power loss. Both types are essential for industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automation where precise positioning is required for optimal performance.</p></p>
