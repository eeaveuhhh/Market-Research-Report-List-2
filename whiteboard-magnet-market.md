<p><p>Whiteboard magnets are commonly available in two main types: double side magnetic whiteboards and single side magnetic whiteboards. Double side magnetic whiteboards offer the convenience of having a magnetic surface on both sides, allowing for more flexibility in displaying materials. On the other hand, single side magnetic whiteboards have a magnetic surface on only one side, making them more suitable for traditional classroom or office settings where only one side is typically used for writing or displaying information. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
