<p><p>Air-cooled screw chillers come in various market types, including electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. In electrodeless adjustment systems, the cooling capacity can be adjusted without the need for physical contact with any parts, making it a more convenient and efficient option. On the other hand, sectional adjustment allows for different sections of the chiller to be adjusted independently, providing greater flexibility and control over the cooling process. Both market types offer unique benefits and cater to different cooling requirements.</p></p>
