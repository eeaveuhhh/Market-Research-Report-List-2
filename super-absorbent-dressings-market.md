<p><p>Super absorbent dressings are used in wound care to promote healing and prevent infection. There are two main types on the market: non-adhering dressings and self-adhering dressings. Non-adhering dressings do not stick to the wound bed, making them easier to remove and less painful for the patient. Self-adhering dressings have an adhesive backing that sticks directly to the skin, providing a secure fit and preventing contamination of the wound. Both types offer high absorbency and are effective in managing exudate from wounds.</p></p>
