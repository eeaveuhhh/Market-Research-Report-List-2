<p><ul><li>Depth-aware Cameras</li><li>Stereo Cameras</li><li>Gesture-based Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gesture Motion Sensor market is divided into three main types - Depth-aware Cameras, Stereo Cameras, and Gesture-based Controllers. Depth-aware Cameras use infrared technology to measure the distance between objects in the field of view, enabling precise gesture recognition. Stereo Cameras use two lenses to create 3D depth perception for accurate detection of hand movements. Gesture-based Controllers are devices that allow users to interact with technology through gestures and motions, such as gaming consoles and VR headsets.</p></p>
