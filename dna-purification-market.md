<p><p>DNA purification market offers various types of purification methods such as alcohol purification, silica gel column based purification, agarose gel-based DNA purification, automated DNA purification, and chemical-based DNA purification. Alcohol purification involves the use of alcohols to precipitate DNA, while silica gel column-based purification uses silica gel columns to isolate DNA. Agarose gel-based DNA purification involves running DNA samples through agarose gels for separation. Automated DNA purification utilizes automated systems for high-throughput DNA purification. Chemical-based DNA purification involves the use of chemicals to extract and purify DNA from samples.</p></p>
