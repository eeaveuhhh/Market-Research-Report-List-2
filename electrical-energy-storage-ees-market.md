<p><p>Electrical Energy Storage (EES) can be classified into three main types based on the mechanism used to store energy: Physical, Electromagnetic, and Chemical. Physical EES systems store energy in the form of mechanical energy, such as compressed air or pumped hydro. Electromagnetic EES systems store energy in magnetic fields, while Chemical EES systems store energy in chemical bonds, like in batteries. Each type of EES market offers different advantages and challenges, catering to various applications in the energy sector.</p></p>
