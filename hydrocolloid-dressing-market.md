<p><p>Hydrocolloid dressings are designed to promote healing in wounds by maintaining a moist environment. Amorphous hydrogel dressings consist of a gel-like substance that conforms to the shape of the wound. Impregnated gauze dressings are gauze sheets infused with hydrogel for easy application. Hydrogel sheets are large, adhesive sheets that provide continuous hydration to the wound. Each type of dressing serves a different purpose in wound care, offering unique benefits depending on the severity and location of the wound.</p></p>
