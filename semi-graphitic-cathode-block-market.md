<p><strong>The Semi-Graphitic Cathode Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Block</li><li>Side Block</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semi-Graphitic Cathode Block market consists of two main types: Bottom Block and Side Block. Bottom Blocks are installed at the bottom of the electrolytic cell to support the cathode. They provide stability and strength to the cathode assembly. Side Blocks, on the other hand, are placed on the sides of the electrolytic cell to connect and hold the cathode blocks together. Both types play a crucial role in the functioning of the cathode assembly in aluminum production.</p></p>
