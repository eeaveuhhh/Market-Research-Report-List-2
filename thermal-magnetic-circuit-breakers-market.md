<p><p>Thermal Magnetic Circuit Breakers are classified into two main types, namely single-pole and multi-pole circuit breakers. Single-pole circuit breakers are designed to protect a single circuit and are commonly used in residential applications. On the other hand, multi-pole circuit breakers are capable of protecting multiple circuits simultaneously and are typically used in commercial or industrial settings. Both types utilize thermal and magnetic mechanisms to quickly interrupt the flow of electricity in case of overload or short circuit, ensuring the safety of electrical systems.</p></p>
