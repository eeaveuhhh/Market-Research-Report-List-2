<p><p>Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) are the two main types of 3D printing of metals market. SLM uses a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powders layer by layer, while EBM uses an electron beam to achieve the same result. Other types of 3D printing of metals market include Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Binder Jetting. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of precision, speed, and material compatibility, catering to a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
