<p><p>Mobile cranes market can be classified into different types based on their functionality and structure. Truck cranes are mounted on trucks for easy transportation around job sites. All-terrain cranes are versatile and can operate on rough terrains as well as highways. Rough terrain cranes are designed for off-road operations in challenging conditions. Crawler cranes use tracks instead of wheels for stability on rough surfaces. Other types of mobile cranes include tower cranes, telescopic cranes, and self-erecting cranes. Each type has its own unique capabilities and advantages in the construction industry.</p></p>
