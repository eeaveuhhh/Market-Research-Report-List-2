<p><p>Grid-connected PV microinverters are commonly used in residential and commercial applications to convert DC power from solar panels into AC power for use in the electrical grid. These microinverters are connected in parallel to each individual solar panel, allowing for maximum efficiency and performance monitoring. On the other hand, grid-off PV microinverters are used in off-grid or standalone systems where there is no connection to the electrical grid. These microinverters can help maximize energy production in remote areas or locations without grid access.</p></p>
