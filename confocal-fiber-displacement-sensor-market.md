<p><p>Confocal fiber displacement sensors are available in two main types - straight type and right-angle type. The straight type sensor is ideal for applications where the measurement target is easily accessible and the sensor can be mounted in a direct line with the target. On the other hand, the right-angle type sensor is suitable for restricted spaces or when the measurement target is not directly accessible. Both types offer precise and accurate displacement measurement capabilities for a variety of industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
