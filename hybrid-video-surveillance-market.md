<p><ul><li>Analog Video Surveillance System</li><li>IP Video Surveillance System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog Video Surveillance Systems use traditional coaxial cables to transmit video signals from cameras to recording devices. They are commonly used in small to medium-sized businesses and offer basic video monitoring capabilities. On the other hand, IP Video Surveillance Systems utilize network infrastructure to transmit high-quality digital video data. They offer advanced features such as remote access, analytics, and integration with other security systems. IP systems are popular in large enterprises and industries requiring comprehensive video surveillance solutions.</p></p>
